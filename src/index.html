Ellen MacArthur

Speaker Spotlight

Dame Ellen MacArthur has enjoyed a successful career, having earned the title of the fastest solo sailor to sail around the world. Having circled the globe, Ellen returned with a new concept of how the world works - including the Earth's finite resources and how our actions as humans are affecting the world around us.
